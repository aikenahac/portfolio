About me

💻 I started my software development journey at the young age of 7 years. Since I grew up in the digital age, I was surrounded by technology my whole life. I had an interest in knowing how the things I entertain myself with daily work. From that point on, every time I used a website or played a game I looked into the background to try to understand how it was built.

🌐 I soon found out I was most interested in web and mobile development, specifically information systems and applications like Facebook, YouTube, and many other popular applications. I started learning by trying to understand how they're built and replicating these applications.

💼 When I came to high school I soon joined my very first startup, YFLab, as a web and mobile developer. I loved working in a team so I grew my horizons and collaborated on multiple startups in diverse teams. I've always preferred a leadership role, so if it was possible I accepted the challenge of leading a team.

📚 In my spare time, I love to help others learn to program. I pass on my mobile development knowledge within the Vegovacraft club at my high school, by teaching a Flutter course available to all students visiting the school.

🎙️ I host a podcast over at hell0.world. We talk about a variety of topics, including software development, space exploration, science, and so much more.
